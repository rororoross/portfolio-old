Redesigning the Milwaukee Urban Gardens Mobile Website

Milwaukee Urban Gardens is a program of Groundwork Milwaukee and a nonprofit land trust dedicated to acquiring and preserving land and partnering with neighborhood residents to develop and maintain community gardens that improve the quality of life.


For a project in my Interaction Design course we had to redesign a local website, specifically the mobile version. I decided on the Milwaukee Urban Gardens site, which lacked any sort of responsive design or mobile presence, The site is a modified WordPress theme that was probably put together years ago, leaving it in a position for a complete refresh.


I created a new logo, simplified the home page and navigation and reorganized the information for a simpler, more mobile-friendly experience.
