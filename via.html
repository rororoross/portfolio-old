Transfer Pizzeria

Web Design & Development

The fullscreen slideshow boasts colorful, high-definition images allowing the client to promote everything from their most recent menu additions to their newest deals. The single page design allows for easy and simple navigation. I designed all of the images myself in Photoshop & developed the site solely with a text editor using HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery. WordPress is implemented in the back-end to allow for the client to easily update every image, menu item, event & special. As with all my work, it is completely responsive, designed to look great on your desktop, tablet or mobile device. Launching in November 2014.
