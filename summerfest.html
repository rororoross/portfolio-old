Summerfest App Concept

Mobile Design

I redesigned the current Summerfest app for my final project in my Interactive Design course. I updated it to a more modern, iOS7 feel. Fixed navigation on the bottom, and an ever-present search bar on the top allows the user to quickly and easily find information about the band they want to see or the stage they want to visit. I fit more information into less pages, creating a more seamless user experience.
